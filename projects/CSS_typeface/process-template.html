CSS Typeface Process

Concept Sketches

I first started with a font I had created the concept for but never made, until now. The idea was to create a legible font out of the spaces of the letters, using dots and lines to outline them.

Letter Sketches

Having chosen my concept, I then chose a grid to form my letters from. I decided on using one of the 3 ways to form a grid shown in class, and marked down the numbers I would need for that method on the grid as well, though some were adjusted after the fact.

Final Letters

Having laid everything out on paper, I then put it into code, and used the grid to form all my characters. Everything was then uploaded to GitHub and made into a page.
