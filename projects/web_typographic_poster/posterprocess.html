CSS Typeface Process

Rough Sketches

I began with a rough idea using a grid, referring back to my typeface, based on a grid. I divided everything into sections to make it easier to code later on, deciding to split it up into divs. Starting with a rough idea and knowing what I wanted, I went straight into coding.

Final Letters

After planning out all the sections, I then worked from the top down. Completing the sections one at a time did mean that I had some difficulty going back to nudge some pieces to fit together better, but overall the project, other than it being a bit fidgety, was alright. Eventually I completed the finishing touches, and finished linking up the sites to their proper urls, and uploaded to GitHub.
